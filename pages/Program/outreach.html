Overview

The Outreach and Networking initiatives at NEST Cluster are designed to bridge the gap between innovation and real-world impact across the North East region. Our mission is to take technological advancements directly to the communities that need them most while building a robust network of industry, academia, and government partners.

Our efforts center on:

  • Community engagement and awareness sessions
  • Regional field visits across diverse landscapes
  • Strategic industry and stakeholder networking
  • Ecosystem building for sustainable growth
  • Knowledge sharing through summits and events

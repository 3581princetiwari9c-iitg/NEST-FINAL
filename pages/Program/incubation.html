Our Incubation Model

The NEST Cluster operates a comprehensive incubation model that bridges the gap between innovative ideas and market-ready startups. Through strategic collaboration between academia, industry, and government, we provide a structured pathway for entrepreneurs across Northeast India.

Anchored at IIT Guwahati and the NEST Hub (IHGT), our ecosystem brings together leading educational institutions, MSMEs, and government bodies to create a thriving innovation landscape.

Startups benefit from access to advanced infrastructure, expert mentorship, funding support, and industry connections at every stage of their journey. This integrated approach ensures the development of innovative, scalable, and sustainable solutions tailored to regional and national needs.

Our Incubation Model for Innovation

Our incubation model is centered at IIT Guwahati, serving as the central nervous system for regional development. We bridge the gap between abstract research and commercial viability.

Innovation Ecosystem

The NEST innovation ecosystem brings together academia, industry, government, and startups to create a collaborative environment for growth and development. Anchored at the NEST Hub and IIT Guwahati, it connects NITs, engineering colleges, diploma colleges, business schools, MSMEs, and schools into a unified network.

This integrated ecosystem enables knowledge exchange, resource sharing, and continuous support for innovators and entrepreneurs. By connecting ideas with expertise and infrastructure, it fosters sustainable solutions and drives technological advancement across the Northeast region.

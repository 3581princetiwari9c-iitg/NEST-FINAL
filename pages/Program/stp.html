Program Overview

Short-Term Programs (STPs) are intensive 5-day / 40-hour learning experiences curated by IIT Guwahati to bridge the gap between academic theory and industry requirements. Designed for students and working professionals, these programs provide immersive, hands-on learning environments with direct industry exposure to cutting-edge technologies and methodologies.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicants must belong to the Northeast region of India
  • Open to students from schools, diploma, and engineering institutions
  • Open to aspiring entrepreneurs and early-stage startups
  • Researchers and innovators with relevant ideas are encouraged to apply
  • MSMEs and industry professionals can participate
  • Basic interest in science, technology, or entrepreneurship is required
  • Willingness to participate actively in training or program activities
  • Applicants should have a problem statement, idea, or learning interest
  • Basic understanding of the chosen domain (preferred but not mandatory)
  • Commitment to complete the duration of the program

👉 Note: Eligibility criteria may vary depending on the specific program or course.

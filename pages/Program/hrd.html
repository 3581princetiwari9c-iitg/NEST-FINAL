Overview

The Human Resource Development initiatives at NEST Cluster are designed to create a skilled, confident, and employable workforce across the region.

Our approach focuses on:

  • Skill development in emerging areas of science and technology
  • Industry-aligned training programmes
  • Hands-on learning through real-world applications
  • Mentorship from academic and industry experts
  • Encouraging entrepreneurship and innovation

By integrating knowledge with practice, NEST ensures that participants are not only trained but are also prepared to contribute effectively in professional and entrepreneurial environments.

Key Offering

Under Human Resource Development, NEST Cluster provides structured training programmes designed to build industry-relevant skills and practical expertise. Through certification programmes, short-term and long-term training, participants gain hands-on experience, technical knowledge, and exposure to real-world applications, enabling them to enhance employability and pursue career or entrepreneurial opportunities.

Certification Program

NEST Cluster offers certification programmes to build industry-relevant technical and professional skills through structured and practical learning, covering areas such as Computer-Aided Design (CAD), Product Design & Innovation, and Business Development.

Short-Term Programmes (STPs)

Short-Term Programmes are intensive modules for rapid skill development, covering Industry 4.0 concepts, product development, innovation and IPR basics, along with hands-on workshops and practical activities.

Long-Term Programmes (LTPs)

Long-Term Programmes provide in-depth learning with mentorship and real-world exposure, including 6-month certifications, internship opportunities up to 9 months, academic and industry guidance, and project-based learning.

Internship Programs

NEST Cluster offers specialized internship opportunities providing hands-on industry exposure and research-driven product development roles to prepare students and professionals for real-world excellence.

3M

Short-Term Internship

Rapid industry exposure designed for undergraduate students. Focuses on foundational skill building, introductory corporate dynamics, and basic hands-on learning within professional settings.

6M

Long-Term Internship

In-depth research and product development roles for students aiming for robust project exposure. Benefit from sustained mentorship, advanced technical modules, and measurable industry milestones.

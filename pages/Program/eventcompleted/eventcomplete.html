Overview

Participate in the Web Development Hackathon to design and build innovative web solutions while tackling real-world challenges. Collaborate with peers, learn from experienced mentors, and showcase your creativity using modern web technologies in a dynamic and competitive environment.

Eligibility

  • Applicants must belong to the Northeast region of India
  • Open to students from schools, diploma, and engineering institutions
  • Open to aspiring entrepreneurs and early-stage startups
  • Researchers and innovators with relevant ideas are encouraged to apply
  • MSMEs and industry professionals can participate
  • Researchers and industry professionals can collaborate as teams

Selection Process

  • Applicants must belong to the Northeast region of India
  • Initial screening of idea submissions based on innovation and feasibility
  • Open to aspiring entrepreneurs and early-stage startups
  • Final presentation and demo before a panel of industry experts

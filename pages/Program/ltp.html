Program Overview

Long-Term Programs (LTPs) are comprehensive learning experiences curated by IIT Guwahati to bridge the gap between academic theory and industry requirements. Designed for students and working professionals, these programs provide immersive, hands-on learning environments with direct industry exposure to cutting-edge technologies and methodologies.

Internship Programs

Gain real-world experience through guided mentorship and hands-on industry exposure. Our long-term internships are specifically architected to provide deep engagement with specific technological and research domains, bridging the gap between student aspiration and industry reality.

Academic Mentorship

Engage in high-impact research projects under the guidance of veteran academicians. Focus on Curriculum-based Knowledge, Research Methodologies, and Skill Development to build a solid academic foundation.

Industrial Mentorship

Experience the professional landscape first-hand. Learn through Live Industrial Projects, Corporate Networking, and Practical Skill Training directly from industry experts.

Hybrid Mentorship

A balanced approach combining theory and practice. Benefit from Flexible Learning Modules, Case Study Analysis, and Continuous Feedback for holistic development.

Certification Programs

Build specialized skills with practical training and industry-recognized certifications. Our certification programs focus on high-demand technical and business competencies that define the modern industrial landscape.

Computer-Aided Design

Excel in modern drafting and simulation. Master 2D & 3D Modeling, Simulation Analysis, and Digital Product Prototyping using industry-standard CAD tools.

Product Design and Innovation

Foster a mindset of creativity and utility. This program covers Design Thinking, User-Centered Innovation, and Prototype Refinement.

Business Development

Drive growth and scalability. Focus on Market Analysis, Financial Planning, and Startup Acceleration Strategies for future leaders.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicants must belong to the Northeast region of India
  • Open to students from schools, diploma, and engineering institutions
  • Open to aspiring entrepreneurs and early-stage startups
  • Researchers and innovators with relevant ideas are encouraged to apply
  • MSMEs and industry professionals can participate
  • Commitment to complete the full duration of the long-term program

👉 Note: Eligibility criteria may vary depending on the specific program or course.

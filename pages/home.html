FAQs

Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers

Who is Eligible for the Program?
Individuals, researchers, startups, and institutions from the North Eastern Region are eligible to participate in the program.
What Support Does the Cluster Offer?
Access to labs, funding, mentorship, networking, and training programs to support innovation and entrepreneurship.
How Can Institutions Participate?
Institutions can join as partners, collaborate on research, and take part in innovation programs.
What Are the Main Objectives?
Promote research, entrepreneurship, skill development, and technology transfer in key sectors.

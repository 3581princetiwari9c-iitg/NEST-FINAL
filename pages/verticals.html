NEST Cluster focuses on key verticals that drive innovation and sustainable development in the Northeast, including Artificial Intelligence, grassroots technologies, bamboo-based products, and eco-friendly waste management solutions.

Innovation Hub on Grassroots Technologies

The Innovation Hub for Grassroots Technologies focuses on developing practical, low-cost solutions tailored to local challenges in the Northeast region. It supports community-driven innovations, promotes traditional knowledge, and enables the transformation of ideas into functional prototypes and sustainable technologies that improve livelihoods and everyday life.

Overview

Founded by a team of atmospheric scientists from IIT Guwahati and data engineers, our venture addresses a critical gap in localized weather forecasting. Current global models lack the granularity required to predict hyper-local flash floods and landslide triggers in the Northeast region. By deploying a proprietary mesh network of low-cost sensors coupled with advanced machine learning models, Aether Dynamics provides actionable insights to agricultural cooperatives and disaster management authorities.
